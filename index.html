About ASM KENYA

ASM KENYA is a premier consulting agency dedicated to connecting talented Kenyan student-athletes with sports scholarship opportunities in the United States.

With over a decade of experience, our team of experts understands the intricate process of college recruitment and scholarship acquisition. We've successfully placed hundreds of Kenyan athletes in NCAA Division I, II, and III programs across the USA.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Kenyan athletic talent and American educational opportunities, providing a pathway to world-class education while pursuing athletic excellence.

Our 5-Step Process

1

Talent Evaluation

We assess your athletic skills, academic performance, and goals to determine scholarship potential.

2

University Matching

We identify the best US college fits based on your profile and preferences.

3

Application Preparation

We help you prepare all necessary documents, highlight reels, and coach recommendations.

4

Coach Connections

We connect you directly with college coaches and arrange recruitment opportunities.

5

Scholarship Acquisition

We negotiate the best possible scholarship package and assist with visa processing.
